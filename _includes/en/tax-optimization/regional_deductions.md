### Regional deductions

Regional deductions are tax deductions established by the autonomous communities of Spain and may vary depending on the
region. These can be used by taxpayers residing in the relevant region if their annual income does not exceed the
established limits. These limits vary by region and deduction category.

Regional deductions can only be applied when submitting the [annual declaration (Renta)](#annual-declaration-renta).

Examples of deductions for Valencia:

- First home purchase under 35 years old.
- Renting a home due to work in another city.
- Investments in renewable energy.
- Eco-donations.
- Donations for cultural heritage.
- Investments in shares of new companies.
- Expenses for educational materials.
- Home improvement works.
- Purchase of new eco-friendly vehicles.
- Expenses for gyms and physical activity.
- Health expenses (dentistry, psychologist, optics, chronic diseases).

In some autonomous communities, starting from 2025, income limits for certain deductions have been raised. For example, in the Valencian Community, for some deductions, the limit was raised to 60,000 euros for individual declarations and 78,000 euros for joint declarations (previously 32,000 and 48,000). For other deductions, the limit remains around 30,000 and 47,000 euros. Be sure to check the current limits for your autonomous community - limits may have changed and new types of deductions may have been added.
