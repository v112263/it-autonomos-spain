## How much tax will need to be paid for the year?

It is very individual - it depends on your income (since the rate is progressive) and on which [tax optimization](#tax-optimization) options you can use.

You can roughly estimate the tax burden (including social contributions) using the free calculator
[autonomoinfo.com](https://autonomoinfo.com/en/){:target="_blank"} - it calculates social contributions and IRPF
taking into account the new contribution system 2023-2031, and you can select your autonomous community and type of autónomo.

If you need a more precise calculation taking into account all variables (deductions, allowances, colaborador, comparison with SL), you can contact a [tax analyst](#tax-analyst).

Once you've estimated your tax burden, you can proceed to [registering as autónomo](#preparation-for-registering-as-autónomo).
