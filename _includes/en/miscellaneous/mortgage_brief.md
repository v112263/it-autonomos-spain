# Mortgage in Spain - step-by-step guide and my personal experience

In November 2024, my wife and I got a mortgage in Valencia for 80% (2.95% annual rate). Despite being autónomo for less than 8 months and not having an annual tax return (renta). The whole process took less than a month and a half - from our first visit to the bank to getting the keys.

**Who this article is for:** residents of Spain - employees, autónomos, Digital Nomad Visa holders, those on Ley Beckham, refugees, and people under temporary protection.

**What's inside:**
- Step-by-step guide from preparation to getting the keys
- My personal week-by-week experience and mistakes you should avoid
- List of documents for the bank
- Government programs to help with your mortgage
- Important things after purchase (refunds, improving your terms)
- Answers to frequently asked questions
